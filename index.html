About me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Muhamad Farhan, also known as Farhan, originally from Palembang. I recently graduated from State Polytechnic of Sriwijaya with a degree in Informatics Management and am currently pursuing my passion for technology as part of the team at PT Pupuk Sriwidjaja.

My journey into the world of programming began in 2018 with C, and since then, I've explored a variety of languages and technologies, including JavaScript, Go, React, Visual Basic .NET, Dart, PHP, HTML, CSS, SQL, NoSQL, Git, and more. Currently, my specialty in software development lies in Go as backend and Next.js as frontend, where I have been honing my skills and expertise.

I've also gained experience with frameworks such as GoFiber, Flutter, Hapi, Node.js, Express, Laravel, CodeIgniter, and Bootstrap, allowing me to develop a robust skill set that I apply to both personal and professional projects. I also have expertise in containerizing applications using Docker and am familiar with Linux environments, enhancing my ability to deploy and manage scalable and efficient solutions.

Aside from programming, I have a strong interest in literature, particularly books and comics, which inspire me creatively and keep me grounded. At PT Pupuk Sriwidjaja, I am dedicated to leveraging my skills to contribute meaningfully to the company's technological advancements and innovations. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I invite you to explore my projects and experiences further.

Professional Project

Property Pusri / Sistem Informasi Rencana dan Pengendalian Aset

This application serves as a comprehensive platform to showcase and manage the identity of SBU Management Assets at PT Pupuk Sriwidjaja. It aims to provide transparency in property leasing and project management within the Asset Management division, facilitating the optimization of asset projects. The application is built upon Next.js for frontend development and utilizes Go (Golang) for backend operations, containerized for efficient deployment and scalability.

SINSHE (Sistem Infromation Safety Health)

This application serves the Department of Safety and Health at PT Pupuk Sriwidjaja, fulfilling various purposes including scheduling pest extermination activities in residential areas. It functions by sending notifications to homeowners to inform them about scheduled pest extermination sessions. The application is developed on Laravel 8 and was initiated during my internship at PT Pupuk Sriwidjaja.

SIMORMARWA

This application, developed for my campus, serves as a platform for managing correspondence between student organizations and campus authorities. It streamlines the scheduling process for student activities in campus buildings and effectively tracks the availability of campus assets. The application is built on Laravel 8 with Firebase as the database infrastructure, ensuring secure and efficient data management.
